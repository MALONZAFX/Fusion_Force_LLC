About Pamela

Pamela Robinson

Pamela Robinson is a keynote speaker, corporate and leadership trainer, and a recognized expert in sales and marketing support for hospitality companies. Her career spans Asia, North America, the United States, and the Caribbean, where she has served as a General Manager, Director of Sales, and consultant for leading hotel operations and hospitality brands.

She is the founder of Fusion Force, a transformational company that specializes in speaking engagements, corporate and leadership training, and sales and marketting support for hospitality businesses seeking to elevate their sales and marketing performance.
